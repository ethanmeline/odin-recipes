Steak Recipe

What you will need:

  • 1 steak
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 sprig of rosemary
  • 1 sprig of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper

Let's get started !

  1. Season the steak with salt and pepper
  2. Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat
  3. Add the steak and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side
  4. Lower the heat and add the butter, garlic, rosemary, and thyme
  5. Baste the steak with the butter and herbs for 1-2 minutes
  6. Remove the steak from the pan and let it rest for 5 minutes
  7. Serve and enjoy!
