Risotto

Classic Risotto Recipe

What you will need:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Let's get started !

  1. In a saucepan, bring the broth to a simmer. Keep warm.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the onion and cook until softened.
  4. Add the rice and cook, stirring, for 2 minutes.
  5. Add the wine and cook, stirring, until absorbed.
  6. Add the broth, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring occasionally until absorbed.
  7. Continue adding broth and stirring until the rice is tender and creamy.
  8. Stir in the Parmesan, the remaining butter, salt, and pepper.
  9. Remove from heat and let stand for 2 minutes before serving.
