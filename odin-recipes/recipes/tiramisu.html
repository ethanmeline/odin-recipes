Tiramisu

Classic Tiramisu Recipe

What you will need:

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pound mascarpone cheese
  • 1/4 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons rum
  • ladyfinger biscuits

Let's get started !

  1. In a medium saucepan, beat together the egg yolks and half of your sugar until well blended.
  2. In a seperate bowl, beat the egg whites and the other half of your sugar until stiff
  3. Then, add the mascarpone and the vanilla to the yellow mixture, and gently mix it in
  4. Add the whipped egg whites and fold those in
  5. In a small bowl, combine coffee and rum. Split ladyfingers in half lengthwise and soak with coffee mixture.
  6. Add a layer of cream, another layer of ladyfingers and finally another layer of cream
  7. Refrigerate for at least 12h before serving
  8. Before serving, sprinkle with cacao powder
